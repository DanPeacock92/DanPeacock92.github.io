Dan Peacock



Dan is a sci-fi and fantasy writer.

His short stories have been published in F&SF, Cast of Wonders, Little Blue Marble, and many other magazines and anthologies. You can find links to them all below. He lives in the UK with several other life forms: his long-suffering partner and daughter, and a second-hand cat that doesn't work properly.

You can contact Dan by email or on Bluesky.
