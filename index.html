Dan Peacock

Dan is a sci-fi and climate fiction writer.

His short fiction has been featured in Nature Magazine, F&SF, Cast of Wonders, and many other magazines and anthologies.

Dan's fiction explores time and space, ecological and societal collapse, dystopian futures, robots with feelings, cats with jobs, and corporate nonsense.

He lives in the UK with several other life forms: his long-suffering partner and daughter, and a cat that doesn't work properly.

Seeking further chaos, Dan runs monthly-ish creative writing workshops for kids in the Books & Bevs bookshop in York, UK.

You can contact Dan on Instagram, Bluesky, or by email.
