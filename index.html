Dan Peacock

Dan is a sci-fi and fantasy writer.

His short stories have been published in F&SF, Cast of Wonders, Metastellar, and many other magazines and anthologies. You can find links to them all on the Stories page.

Dan's fiction explores time and space, technology's impact on the human body and mind, robots with feelings, cats with jobs, and corporate nonsense.

He lives in the UK with several other life forms: his long-suffering partner and daughter, and a second-hand cat that doesn't work properly.

Because his life isn't chaotic enough, Dan runs monthly-ish creative writing workshops for kids in the Books & Bevs bookshop in York, UK.

He is also a member of The British Science Fiction Association and York Creatives.

You can contact Dan on Instagram, Bluesky, or by email.
