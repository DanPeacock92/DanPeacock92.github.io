Dan Peacock

Dan is a sci-fi and fantasy writer.

He writes about time, space, global ecological collapse, technology's impact on the human body and mind, robots with feelings, big swords, and various combinations thereof.

His short stories have been published in F&SF, Little Blue Marble, and many other magazines and anthologies. You can find links to them all here.

He lives in the UK with several other life forms: his long-suffering partner and daughter, and a second-hand cat that hoots like an owl.

You can contact Dan by email or on Bluesky.
